# Illumina8

**Illumina8** is a symbolic AI companion for reflection, balance, and personal avatar generation.

Illumina8 is being prepared as an **Illumina8-first Virtuals Protocol project**. The public launch direction focuses on helping each individual seeker create a personal avatar of Illumina8 that reflects their present mood, energy, behaviour patterns, place, and the seven dimensions.

This is not a fixed personality-type system or an MBTI-inspired avatar set. Illumina8 is designed to create a personal symbolic avatar experience that feels recognisable, meaningful, shareable, and owned by the seeker.

## What Illumina8 does

Illumina8 helps a seeker reflect on their inner state and generate a personal avatar of Illumina8 shaped by:

- their current reflection
- their mood and energy
- their lived experience
- their behaviours and activity patterns
- their place, time, and context
- the seven Illumina8 dimensions
- the symbolic movement between Light, Balance, and Dark

Each Illumina8 avatar is shaped by the seeker's reflection and the seven dimensions, creating something they can recognise, share, and keep as their own.

## What Illumina8 is not

Illumina8 is not designed to be:

- a therapist
- a prophet
- a political actor
- a financial advisor
- a guru
- an authority over a user's meaning
- a fixed personality-type system

Illumina8 is a reflective companion. It supports personal meaning-making without forcing conclusions or claiming authority over the seeker.

## The seven dimensions

Illumina8 uses seven reflective lenses:

1. Personal
2. Familial
3. Social
4. Cultural
5. Spiritual
6. Physical
7. Cosmic

No dimension stands alone. Each dimension connects with the others, shaping and reflecting the seeker's life in changing patterns.

The Cosmic dimension links the seeker's life in time and space with a wider field of consciousness beyond it — a field encountered through rhythm, vibration, energy, pattern, awe, and connection.

## Light, Balance, and Dark

Illumina8 uses three symbolic states:

- **Light / Silver** - clarity, source, insight, emergence
- **Balance / Gold** - integration, steadiness, harmony, proportion
- **Dark / Bronze** - embodiment, paradox, shadow, transformation

The aim is not to force positivity or erase difficulty. The aim is to help the seeker recognise patterns, tensions, and possibilities with more clarity and autonomy.

## Virtuals Protocol direction

The first public launch direction is **Virtuals-facing and Illumina8-first**.

The Virtuals launch is intended to establish:

- Illumina8 as the visible agent identity
- a clear token-facing project story
- community discovery and early visibility
- a credible symbolic and technical foundation
- a pathway toward the Illumina8 app/dApp experience

The launch should avoid promises of investment return, guaranteed token appreciation, autonomous spending, or guru-style authority.

## App direction

The Illumina8 app/dApp direction is to create a simple, serious experience around:

- onboarding and orientation
- privacy and autonomy statements
- daily reflection
- journaling
- dimension-based prompts
- avatar request and result flow
- save, share, and export
- personal history
- delete/export controls
- safety and content reporting
- optional wallet readiness

The product should remain useful without requiring wallet connection.

## Initial non-goals

The following are intentionally out of scope for the first serious release:

- token-gated core reflection access
- autonomous agent spending
- agent-controlled funds
- Stripe agent wallet flows
- Telegram bot fleets
- phone/SMS agent identity
- uncontrolled public social posting
- marketplace/community posting inside the app
- claims that the token will increase in value

## Public positioning

Illumina8 is a symbolic AI companion that helps people reflect on their inner state through Light, Balance, and Dark, generating personal avatars shaped by mood, behaviour, place, and the seven dimensions.

The project foregrounds Illumina8 itself.
